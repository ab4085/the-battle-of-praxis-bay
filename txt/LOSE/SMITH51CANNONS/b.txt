These past few weeks had been an absolute blast on board this upside-down ship. The design was fresh, new, like a cooling hand passed over your brow as compared with the boring monotony of every other ship of the sea. Your crew members often hailed you as a genius for seeming to know this ship inside-out when they were having trouble even finding the privy. But the truth couldn't be further, she still kept many secrets from you, and you were far from ready to fight a proper battle, much less one against a ship of the calibre you're up against, or so you've heard. Now is not the time for sneaking above deck to take a peek yourself, and the weak light of morning would probably prevent you from seeing anyway. Now is the time to prepare for battle.
