Your enemies are bearing down on you as your own crew scrambles to prepare for a fight. But how will you fight? This is your first ever engagement aboard this unusual vessel, not the ideal situation given what you're up against. The safest option would be to prepare for a majority boarding - there's not time for the crew to figure out the guns on this thing, so it would be better to just stick to what you know, and try and pressure the enemy to give up from their own vessel.
