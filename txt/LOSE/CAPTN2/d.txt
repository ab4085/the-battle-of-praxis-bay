It occurs to you that you, a mere pirate, are being pursued by the mightiest ship of the line Hildonia has at its disposal. This is most certainly not a usual response to losing a single ship to pirates. If Hildonia were so desperate to reclaim their vessel that they would rushedly dispatch such a ship as the Python, it makes you wonder about what further mysteries are lurking within this prototype ship. But the time for wondering is no more. Now is the time to fight.
