You've never seen so many injuries in all your years. Your life as a pirate surgeon was quite an easy one, the captain always seemed to know of the least violent course of action to take no matter the engagement or circumstance. But today, the tides have changed. The stench of blood hangs in the air, and the bodies are starting to pile up. You're doing what you can to keep the remainder of the crew fit for fighting, but regardless of what's going on up there, you're fighting a losing battle down here. There has only been one ray of hope that has glimmered through the horrors of the battle so far: only lesser crew members seemed to be receiving injuries.
