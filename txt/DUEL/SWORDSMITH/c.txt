And what an intervention you get. The ground inches behind your enemy erupts as a cannon shot flies out from under the deck. From the angle, it would appear some genius from aboard your own ship decided to fire a cannon shot almost directly upwards. A brain-dead decision in most scenarios, but this shot seemed to perfectly pierce the mainmast, bringing it crashing down. Your enemy leaps out of the way of the felling mast, which unfortunately for him is directly in the direction of your sword.
