About an hour ago, you were enjoying some hard-earned sleep. While the most of those other useless larks were slacking off, you'd done your duties and had done them as well as you'd always done serving under Captain Carlos. You've known him years longer than anybody else aboard this vessel. But with what you've heard of the news above deck, it looks like that time may come to an abrupt end. You hastily prepare for what is easily going to be the hardest-fought battle in your wretched life.
