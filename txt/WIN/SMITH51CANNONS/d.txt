If you have the man-power, you could order the men to congregate the powder to be able to return fire better, but you lose the advantage of the ship's design, and wasting the crew's time looking for the powder only to just move it somewhere else might be your undoing. You could help direct the powder relocation, but that would take your attention from maintaining the ship's repairs, an even greater risk. Then what say you leave the powder where it is, and leave the men to find it themselves? Keeping yourselves afloat isn't exactly a low priority; it demands a lot of your attention.
