You were aware that you were fighting a battleship, but the towering sight of the Python, the flagship of the Hildonia navy caught you in speechless surprise. But what was somehow more surprising still was to see the darling of Hildonia's fleet fighting a losing battle against pirates. The Python was in a state greatly unbefitting a boat of her stature. You could see multiple fires roaring with men struggling to put them out, most of her guns weren't even firing, and you could swear it looked like she was sinking. You quickly spot some injured crewmates on deck and get to work. It looks like there's much to do.
