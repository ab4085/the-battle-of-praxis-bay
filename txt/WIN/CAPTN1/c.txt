Every ship on the seven seas is almost exactly the same in its operation. Some are larger and require more crew, but this one is different. A revolution in ship design, even having captained her for a couple weeks now, you still find yourself at times getting lost and losing your bearings just by simply wandering around. She's supposedly meant to revolutionise combat, but you have not yet had the chance to see what she can do, and during her capture her crew surrendered without a fight. Being aboard a vessel of such mystery gives you mild discomfort as her captain, but even this doesn't detract from your mood so much as to dishearten you from your string of recent successes.
